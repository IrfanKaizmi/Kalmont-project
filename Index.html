About us

A dedicated group committed to safety and solidarity. With expertise and compassion, we navigate challenges to safeguard mountain enthusiasts. Unyielding support, always

Professional Rescue, Safeguarding Lives

Snow Mountain Rescue Team employs state-of-the-art techniques and training for high-altitude rescue. With skilled mountaineers and advanced equipment, we swiftly respond to emergencies, ensuring the prompt and safe rescue of those in distress. Our dedicated team is committed to safeguarding climbers, serving as a reliable beacon in mountain crises.

Adventure Journey Safety Guide

Our comprehensive safety guide covers avalanche awareness, cold weather tips, emergency response plans, high-altitude considerations, equipment essentials, and emergency contacts for a secure mountain experience.

Unveiling the Mysteries of Avalanches

Our Avalanche Safety Guide provides insights into avalanche formation, risk identification based on terrain and snow characteristics, and emphasizes prevention through hazard avoidance and understanding local avalanche forecasts. Stay informed, stay safe in the mountains.

Explore our rescue organization's activities

From specialized training sessions to community outreach events, we actively engage in fostering safety awareness, preparedness, and building strong connections. Join us in our commitment to a safer and resilient community.
