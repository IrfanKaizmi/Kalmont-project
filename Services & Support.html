Data Analysis and Prediction

We are dedicated to executing safe and effective avalanche rescue missions using cutting-edge technology and expertise. Here is an overview of our avalanche risk assessment and prediction process:

Avalanche Probability Analysis

We assess potential avalanche risk areas through in-depth analysis of terrain data and snow layer characteristics, utilizing advanced Geographic Information System (GIS) tools. This includes factors such as altitude, slope, terrain shape, as well as snow layer thickness, structure, and stability.

Consideration of Meteorological Conditions

We closely monitor meteorological conditions, including snowfall, wind direction, and temperature. These factors are crucial for evaluating snowpack stability and potential risks and are also used in predicting future weather conditions.

Model Development

Utilizing avalanche dynamics models, we consider various factors such as terrain, snow layer characteristics, and meteorological conditions to build models that assess the probability of avalanche occurrence.

Risk Assessment

By combining data and model results, we conduct a comprehensive risk assessment to identify potential hazard areas and likelihood. This aids in prioritizing search and rescue missions, ensuring optimal resource allocation.
