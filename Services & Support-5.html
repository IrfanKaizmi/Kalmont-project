Services & Support

Effective avalanche rescue involves advanced data analysis, digital equipment, and well-trained search dogs. Rescue teams employ specialized tools, utilize helicopter support in remote areas, and include medical personnel for swift and comprehensive operations. Collaboration with local authorities and volunteers enhances support and manpower, ensuring efficient and successful rescue missions.

Collaborative Rescue

We believe in the power of collaboration, recognizing that effective rescue operations require a unified effort. Our approach involves working closely with local authorities, militia, and dedicated volunteers to pool resources and expertise.

Local Authorities

Coordination with local government and emergency services ensures seamless integration of our rescue efforts with existing response mechanisms. This collaboration enhances overall efficiency and facilitates swift decision-making.

Militia Involvement

Engaging local militia contributes valuable on-the-ground support. Their familiarity with the terrain and community aids in navigating challenging environments, and their assistance bolsters the manpower available for rescue operations.

Volunteer Support

Volunteers play a crucial role, providing additional manpower and support. Trained individuals from the community contribute their time and skills to reinforce our rescue teams, enabling a more extensive reach and coverage.

Resource Pooling

Collaborative rescue operations allow for the pooling of resources, equipment, and knowledge. This collective effort ensures a comprehensive and well-coordinated response, maximizing the chances of successful rescues.
